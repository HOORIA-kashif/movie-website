venom

The Venom movie, released in 2018, is a superhero film set in the Marvel Universe, focusing on the popular anti-hero Venom. Directed by Ruben Fleischer, the film stars Tom Hardy as Eddie Brock, an investigative journalist who becomes the host of an alien symbiote named Venom. This symbiote gives Eddie incredible powers, along with a dark, chaotic alter ego that has its own personality, cravings, and violent impulses.
